Al Raouche Rocks

Off the coast of Raouché, there is a natural landmark called the Pigeons' Rock (also known as the Rock of Raouché), consisting of two rock formations.

Byblos Citadel

Byblos Castle is a Crusader castle in Byblos, Lebanon. In Crusader times it was known as the Castle of Gibelet.

Beiteddine Palace

Beiteddine Palace is an 18th-century palace in Beiteddine, Lebanon, built by Bashir II.
